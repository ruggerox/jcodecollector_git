jCodeCollector 2.5 for LINUX

How to launch jCC

- open a shell 
- move into jCodeCollector folder
- type ./launch_jcc.sh (or "java -jar jcodecollector.jar -Dawt.useSystemAAFontSettings=o")
- have fun!

(jCC database default location is ~/.jCodeCollector)
